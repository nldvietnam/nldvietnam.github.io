Technical Advisory Team meeting December 2016
Date: 20/12/2016
On 20th Dec 2016, National Legislative Development Project (NLD) and International Cooperation Department of Ministry of Justice (ICD, MOJ) organized a Technical Advisory Team meeting (TAT meeting). The meeting was co-chaired by Ms. Kimberly Inksater – NLD Project Director and Ms. Tran Thu Huong – Deputy Director General of ICD, MOJ. Representatives from partner agencies, including Ministry of Justice, Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Industry and Trade, Office of the Government and Office of National Assembly.

In the presentation on the project progress, Ms. Kimberly Inksater informed all participants that until 15th Dec 2016, in year 2 Phase 2 of the Project, despite some constraints in project procedures, 75% of all activities were completed or under completion.

Representatives of partners also informed the progress of activities in a positive way. Partners committed to quickly conduct unimplemented activities before the end of the fiscal year 2016-2017 of the project.

One of the most outstanding activities of the Project is the study mission to Canada with the participation of officials from Ministry of Finance and Ministry of Justice. Head of mission delegation – Mr. Dang Cong Khoi, Deputy Director General of Ministry of Finance had a presentation on the content of discussion and experience on budget allocation, policy impact assessment, cost-effectiveness analysis, monitoring law implementation.

Ms. Tran Thu Huong informed participants of the situation of legal cooperation, collecting proposals from partners, timeline to send official proposals to NLD and introduced criteria to select activities.

Mr. Jacob Gammelgaard – NLD Project Field Manager notified that NLD would revise the procedure for recruitment of consultants by applying the public tender process where qualified consultants will submit their applications as individual candidates. This process would help ensure the transparency of the selection of consultants and broaden the application coverage and improve the quality of consultants to be recruited for project activities.

To end the meeting, parties agreed to quickly implement remaining activities of Annual Workplan 2016-2017 and develop Annual Workplan 2017-2018.
